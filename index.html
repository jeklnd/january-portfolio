About

I found myself appreciating beautiful websites and apps so much that in 2022 I started building them.

Now an appreciation of the eye has grown into an obsession of the fingers over writing well-structured, semantic HTML, modern, terse CSS, and readable, modular JavaScript. I learn new libraries and frameworks reguarly, and most importantly, I adapt to whatever tools are required for job.

I'm based in Philadelphia, PA, but I'm happy working remotely and have experience working in remote teams.

When I'm not coding, you can find me travelling and working out. I'm looking for work and would love to connect.
